# AI Economic Impact Dashboard

A free, open-source interactive dashboard tracking jobs displaced by artificial intelligence since ChatGPT's launch on November 30, 2022.

**Live site:** [ai-tech-nerd.github.io/ai-economic-impact-dashboard](https://ai-tech-nerd.github.io/ai-economic-impact-dashboard/)

## What's inside

- **Dashboard** — Total jobs displaced with breakdowns by company, industry, job type, and trend over time
- **Predictions** — Which job categories face the highest AI displacement risk over the next 3 months to 5 years
- **Timeline** — Slider and story modes that animate displacement events over time, starting with ChatGPT's growth to 100M users
- **AI Advancements** — 207 milestones: model releases, launches, partnerships, regulations across 12 countries, and breakthroughs
- **AI Companies** — Profiles and milestone timelines for 27 companies including OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and Meta
- **Learn & Prepare** — Free AI tools, the CRAFT prompt framework, a 30-day action plan, and curated courses

## Data integrity

Every data point links to its original source — company statements, earnings calls, or credible reporting. Events are cross-referenced with research including [Anthropic's labor market impact analysis](https://www.anthropic.com/research/the-economy) to distinguish AI-driven displacement from pandemic-era over-hiring corrections.

Currently tracking **21 verified events** across **102,000+ jobs** affected by AI adoption.
