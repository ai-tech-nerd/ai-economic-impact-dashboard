## SECTION 5: How the Data Works

### Heading (H2)
**Methodology and transparency**

### Body Copy
Every displacement event in this dashboard meets three criteria:

1. **Company-attributed** — The company publicly stated the job cuts were related to AI adoption
2. **Documented** — Each event links to earnings calls, press releases, SEC filings, or credible reporting
3. **Verified count** — Job numbers come from official announcements, not estimates

Predictions are clearly separated from verified data and are based on: (1) extrapolation of current trends, (2) announced company AI adoption plans, (3) industry analyst reports, and (4) academic research.

This is an MVP. Data is being continuously verified and expanded. If you spot an error or have a correction, [reach out](link-to-contact) — community accuracy matters.
