# Timelines (initial launch + major developments) — through March 17, 2026

## Perplexity
- Dec 2022 — Perplexity launches its “answer engine” web product.
- Apr 5, 2023 — Raises Series A and launches iOS app.
- May 30, 2024 — Launches “Pages” (report-style outputs / structured write-ups).
- Jan 23, 2025 — Launches Perplexity Assistant for Android.
- Jun 2024 / Jan 2025 — Expands voice/assistant capabilities on mobile (Android first; iOS later as permissions allow).
- Jul 2, 2025 — Launches a $200/month “Max” tier.
- 2025–2026 — Partnerships bundling Perplexity Pro (examples: PayPal/Venmo offer; Airtel India offer).
- Feb 5, 2026 — Introduces “Model Council” (multi-model comparison feature).

## Inflection AI
- 2022 — Inflection AI founded.
- May 2, 2023 — Launches Pi (Personal AI) assistant.
- Mar 19, 2024 — Microsoft hires Mustafa Suleyman + Karén Simonyan and much of the team; Inflection pivots away from consumer Pi focus toward enterprise/commercial strategy; Inflection-2.5 mentioned as available via Azure in reporting.

## xAI
- Jul 12, 2023 — xAI publicly announced by Elon Musk.
- Nov 3, 2023 — Grok announced (xAI’s first flagship chatbot/model family).
- Mar 17, 2024 — Grok-1 weights/architecture open-released by xAI.
- Aug 14–20, 2024 — Grok-2 rolls out on X with image generation (early/gradual rollout).
- Feb 18–20, 2025 — Grok 3 announced/rolled out (flagship model update; access broadened over time).
- Apr 10, 2025 — xAI launches API access for Grok 3 (pricing + developer access).
- 2025 — Standalone Grok app expands globally (iOS first; Android rollout follows).

## Grok (product/app)
- Nov 3, 2023 — Grok announced.
- Dec 6, 2024 — Grok made available broadly on X (wider access).
- Jan 9, 2025 — Standalone Grok iOS app launches in the U.S. (after earlier limited testing).
- Feb 4, 2025 — Standalone Grok Android app announced/rolled out (initially limited regions, then wider).
- Mar–Apr 2025 — Adds image editing + deeper search features; launches Grok 3 API.

## DeepSeek
- Dec 2024 — DeepSeek V3 (initial) released (per reporting).
- Jan 20, 2025 — DeepSeek chatbot app launches (based on R1) on iOS/Android.
- Mar 24–25, 2025 — DeepSeek V3-0324 released (MIT License; reported as major upgrade).
- May 28, 2025 — DeepSeek R1-0528 released (MIT License).
- Aug 21, 2025 — DeepSeek V3.1 released (MIT License; “thinking/non-thinking” hybrid positioning in summaries).

## Apple Intelligence
- Jun 10, 2024 — Apple Intelligence announced at WWDC 2024 (Apple’s personal intelligence system).
- Sep 9, 2024 — Apple confirms first rollout begins with iOS 18.1 / iPadOS 18.1 / macOS Sequoia 15.1 “next month.”
- Oct 28, 2024 — Apple Intelligence first features available with iOS 18.1 / iPadOS 18.1 / macOS 15.1.
- 2024–2025 — Additional features ship in follow-on updates (incremental expansion over time).

## Manus
- Mar 6, 2025 — Manus AI agent officially launches (Butterfly Effect Pte. Ltd.).
- Apr 2025 — Major funding attention (reported).
- Dec 29–30, 2025 — Meta announces acquisition of Manus (reported valuation range $2–3B in coverage).
- Oct 16, 2025 — Manus 1.5 noted as a major version update (per public listings).
