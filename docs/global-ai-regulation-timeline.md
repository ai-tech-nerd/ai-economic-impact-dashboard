# Global AI regulation timeline (proposed → enacted → removed/withdrawn) → March 17, 2026

This timeline focuses on **major, widely-cited AI governance instruments** (hard law + key government rules) and includes **proposal/adoption/effective** dates and **withdrawals/revocations** where applicable.

## Timeline

| Date (UTC) | Jurisdiction / Body | Instrument | Status event | What happened (high-level) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Feb 18, 2016 | United States (Google Cloud) | Cloud Vision API (policy surface via API terms/usage controls) | **Public beta** | Early large-scale computer-vision service offered to developers/businesses (not a dedicated AI law, but a major regulated-use surface via cloud policy). |
| Jan 23, 2019 | Singapore | Model AI Governance Framework (1st ed.) | **Issued** | National voluntary governance framework released (accountability-based). |
| May 22, 2019 | OECD | OECD AI Principles | **Adopted** | First intergovernmental AI principles adopted by OECD members (soft law). |
| Jan 21, 2020 | Singapore | Model AI Governance Framework (2nd ed.) | **Issued** | Updated framework released with expanded guidance and examples (soft law). |
| Apr 21, 2021 | European Union | EU Artificial Intelligence Act (AI Act) | **Proposed** | European Commission proposes a risk-based AI regulation (hard law path begins). |
| Nov 23, 2021 | UNESCO | Recommendation on the Ethics of AI | **Adopted** | Global ethical standard adopted by UNESCO Member States (soft law). |
| Dec 31, 2021 | China | Algorithmic Recommendation Provisions | **Issued** | China issues binding rules for algorithmic recommendation services. |
| Mar 1, 2022 | China | Algorithmic Recommendation Provisions | **Effective** | Algorithmic recommendation rules take effect. |
| Jun 2022 | Canada | Bill C-27 (includes AIDA) | **Introduced / proposed** | Canada tables Bill C-27 including the Artificial Intelligence and Data Act (AIDA) as proposed national AI law. |
| Sep 28, 2022 | European Union | AI Liability Directive (AILD) | **Proposed** | EU proposes procedural liability rules addressing proof/disclosure issues for AI-related tort claims. |
| Nov 25, 2022 | China | Deep Synthesis Provisions (deepfakes, synthetic media) | **Issued** | China issues binding “deep synthesis” rules (labeling, security, governance). |
| Jan 10, 2023 | China | Deep Synthesis Provisions | **Effective** | Deep synthesis rules take effect. |
| 2021 (enacted) / Jan 1, 2023 (took effect) / Jul 5, 2023 (enforcement) | United States (New York City) | Local Law 144 (AEDT bias audits & notices) | **Enacted / effective / enforced** | NYC establishes audit + notice requirements for automated employment decision tools (AEDTs); enforcement starts July 5, 2023. |
| Mar 29, 2023 | United Kingdom | “AI regulation: a pro-innovation approach” White Paper | **Proposed (policy framework)** | UK publishes a regulator-led approach (principles + sector regulators) rather than a single AI statute. |
| Mar 15, 2024 | India | MeitY AI Advisory (March 2024) | **Revised / softened** | India revises its advisory after industry concern, removing an “approval before deployment” mandate for AI models (policy change / partial rollback). |
| Mar 13, 2024 | European Union | EU AI Act | **Adopted (Parliament)** | European Parliament adopts the AI Act. |
| May 21, 2024 | European Union | EU AI Act | **Adopted (Council)** | Council formally adopts the AI Act. |
| May 17, 2024 | United States (Colorado) | SB24-205 “Consumer Protections for Artificial Intelligence” | **Signed** | Colorado enacts a comprehensive “high-risk AI” consumer protection law (algorithmic discrimination focus). |
| Mar 13, 2024 | United States (Utah) | SB149 “Artificial Intelligence Amendments” | **Signed** | Utah enacts AI-focused consumer protection disclosures (notably “chatbot/AI disclosure” requirements). |
| May 1, 2024 | United States (Utah) | SB149 | **Effective** | Utah AI consumer protection amendments take effect. |
| Jul 12, 2024 | European Union | EU AI Act | **Published** | AI Act published in the EU Official Journal. |
| Aug 1, 2024 | European Union | EU AI Act | **Entered into force** | AI Act enters into force (staggered application timelines begin). |
| Sep 29, 2024 | United States (California) | SB 1047 (frontier AI safety bill) | **Vetoed (removed from enactment path)** | Governor vetoes SB 1047; it does not become law. |
| Dec 10, 2024 | Brazil | Bill 2338/2023 (Brazil AI bill) | **Approved by Senate (still pending)** | Senate approves major AI bill; moves to Chamber of Deputies (not yet enacted). |
| Dec 26, 2024 | South Korea | AI Basic Act | **Passed** | National Assembly passes the AI Basic Act (comprehensive national framework). |
| Jan 6, 2025 | Canada | Bill C-27 (incl. AIDA) | **Died (removed from consideration)** | Bill C-27 dies when Parliament is prorogued (legislative process ends without enactment). |
| Jan 23, 2025 | United States (Federal) | Executive Order 14110 (AI safety EO) | **Revoked** | A new order explicitly references the revocation of EO 14110 and initiates review/rescission of actions taken under it. |
| Feb 2025 | European Union | AI Liability Directive (AILD) | **Withdrawal announced** | European Commission work programme indicates the AILD proposal will be withdrawn due to lack of foreseeable agreement. |
| Oct 6, 2025 | European Union | AI Liability Directive (AILD) | **Officially withdrawn** | Notice of withdrawal is published in the EU Official Journal (AILD proposal formally withdrawn). |
| Feb 1, 2026 | United States (Colorado) | SB24-205 | **Effective** | Colorado’s high-risk AI obligations take effect. |
| Jan 2026 (implemented) | South Korea | AI Basic Act | **Effective / implemented** | South Korea implements the AI Basic Act (with a grace period described in reporting). |
