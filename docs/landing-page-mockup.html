The information gap is the real risk

The biggest threat from AI isn't the technology itself — it's not knowing what's happening until it affects you directly.

Headlines swing between "AI will take all jobs" and "AI is just a tool." Neither is useful. What's useful is seeing the actual data: which companies have made cuts, how many roles were affected, and what the trajectory looks like.

This dashboard was built as a companion to The AI Shift — a practical guide to understanding AI's workforce impact and preparing for it. The dashboard provides the data. The guide provides the strategy.

Methodology and transparency

Every displacement event in this dashboard meets three criteria:

  • Company-attributed — The company publicly stated the job cuts were related to AI adoption
  • Documented — Each event links to earnings calls, press releases, SEC filings, or credible reporting
  • Verified count — Job numbers come from official announcements, not estimates

Predictions are clearly separated from verified data and are based on current trends, announced company AI adoption plans, industry analyst reports, and academic research.

This is an MVP. Data is being continuously verified and expanded. If you spot an error or have a correction, the community's accuracy matters.
