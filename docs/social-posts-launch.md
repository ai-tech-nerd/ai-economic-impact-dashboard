## LinkedIn

You've seen the headlines. Block cuts 40% of its workforce. Amazon trims 30,000 roles. Salesforce replaces 4,000 support positions with AI.

Each story shows up for a day, gets a few comments, and disappears. The next one lands a week later. Then another. Then another.

The pattern is there, but no one's putting the full picture in one place.

That bothered me enough to build something about it.

I took everything from my posts and articles about AI's economic and psychological impact and turned it into a live, interactive dashboard:
https://ai-tech-nerd.github.io/ai-economic-impact-dashboard/

What's in it:
→ 21 verified displacement events tracking 102,000+ jobs
→ 207 AI milestones — model releases, partnerships, regulations across 12 countries
→ 27 AI company profiles with per-company timelines
→ Predictions showing which job categories face the highest risk
→ An animated timeline that walks through the story from ChatGPT's launch to now
→ A learning section with free courses and frameworks to help people prepare

Every data point links to its original source. Company statements, earnings calls, credible reporting. No aggregated claims without attribution.

This is an MVP. There's a lot of dates to verify and data to add. If you spot something that's off — a wrong date, a missing event, a number that doesn't match the source — let me know so it can be updated.

Free, open source, no sign-up: https://ai-tech-nerd.github.io/ai-economic-impact-dashboard/
